My current research explores human-oriented design approaches to guide the responsible development and deployment of AI-based systems, ensuring they remain people-centred and under human decision-making. I also investigate how to equip city managers and planners with interdisciplinary competencies to successfully deliver digital innovation projects, including AI initiatives in the public sector. My past research focused on developing smart city architectures that enable urban planners to align citizens' needs with digital solutions.
